FAQ

What is Momentum Score?
Momentum Score (0-100) shows how well a token historically performed during bull phases.
How is a bull phase defined?
A bull phase starts when BTC shows >5% growth in 24h, continues while growth is >2%, and ends when growth drops below 2%.
What is Beta?
Beta shows how much a token grows relative to BTC on average.
What is Consistency?
Consistency shows in what percentage of bull phases the token was in the top 20% performers.
